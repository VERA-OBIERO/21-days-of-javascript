LINKIN PARK

This is a rock band from Agoura hills in California. They were initially known as Xero (1996-1998) then later Hybrid Theory (1999-2000) and finally Linkin Park.

The band consists of five members namely:

  • Mike Shinoda
  • Brad Delson
  • Rob Bourdon
  • Joe Hahn
  • Dave Farrell

The band also consisted of Chester Bennington who unfortunately passed away in July 2017.
